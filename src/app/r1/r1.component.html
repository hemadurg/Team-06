About Radisson Blu Resort Visakhapatnam



Set close to Visakhapatnam Airport and Kailasagiri train station, Radisson Blu Resort boasts luxury accommodations. The property houses sophisticated ocean-view rooms, a posh ambiance, hot tubs, and beauty services. Take relaxing swims at the infinity pool, relish gourmet meals, receive specialized massages at the spa, and enjoy activities like cycling, billiards, and BBQs. With Sagar Nagar and Rushikonda beaches at your doorstep, lose yourself in the serenity of Visakhapatnam here.
